Double and half

Home

This trick generalizes many other tricks and can be used to simplify a problem to a known trick.

The Trick:

One number is halved and the other is doubled in a multiplication problem, and the answer remains the same.

Examples:

Find 45 × 18

  1. 45 × 2 = 90. 18 × 2 = 9.
  2. 9 × 90 = 810. The answer is 810.

Find 22 × 82

  1. 22 ÷ 2 = 11. 82 × 2 = 164.
  2. 11 × 164 = 1,804, by using multiplication by 11.

Find 162⁄3 × 84

This time we will multiply and divide by 6 instead of 2.
  1. 162⁄3 × 6 = 100. 84 ÷ 6 = 14.
  2. 100 × 14 = 1,400.
