Multiply by 50

This trick is the same in principle as multiplying by 5. Except now we will relate 50 to 100.

The Trick:

100 = 50 × 2 so do the following
  1. Half the number.
  2. Multiply the result by 100 and write it down.
Note that all odd numbers will end in 50 and all even numbers will end in 00.

Examples

Find 161 × 50

  1. Half of 161 is 80.5.
  2. 80.5 × 100 is 8050. The answer is 8050.

Find 1,092 × 50

  1. Half of 1,092 is 546.
  2. 546 × 100 = 54,600. The answer is 54,600.
