Multiply by 75

Home

Think of 75 as 3 × 100 ÷ 4.

The Trick:

  1. Divide the number by 4 and remember the remainder.
  2. Multiply the whole part of the result by 3 and then multiply by 100.
  3. Add 75 × the remainder to the result. Write it down.
Note that all odd numbers will end in 25 or 75 and all even numbers will end in 00 or 50.

Examples

Find 124 × 75

  1. 124 ÷ 4 = 31 with remainder 0.
  2. 31 × 300 = 9300. The answer is 9,300.

Find 1,091 × 50

  1. 1,091 ÷ 4 = 272 with a remainder of 3.
  2. 272 × 300 = 81,600.
  3. Add 225 to the result to get 81,825.
